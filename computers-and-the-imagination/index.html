Computers and the Imagination

The sketches in this folder are based on the book "Computers and the Imagination" by Clifford A. Pickover.

  • butterfly01 is just a p5 version of the pseudocode on page 21
  • butterfly02 basically the same as 01 but with lines instead of points to get a decent image, with fewer steps and with translation to set the position.
  • butterfly03 a little rotation and using trangles to fill the wings with color
